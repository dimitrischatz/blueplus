Engagement

Meeting critical transaction parameters with confidence - Ensuring safety, transparency, scalability, and compliance.

Transaction Prerequisites

Each transaction must fulfill the following critical parameters:


•  Safety
•  Transparency
•   Scalability
•   Compliance 

Our Terms

•  All transactions have to be clear from operational, credibility, AML, red flags, etc. We place extra emphasis on the above three risks, especially AML.

•  Precious metals concentrate mining must comply with the UN and OECD regulations, and all mining concentrates must not be products of human and environmental exploitation.

•  Sourcing and trade at all times shall observe AML regulations.

•  We source opportunities associated with physical flows of commodities trading.

•  We look only for transactions that are recurring, with the parties involved having significant track record in the category of the proposed transaction.

•  We do not seek one-off transactions, nor transactions originating from parties that are not related to the corresponding business, either in scope or scale.
