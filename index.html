Engagement

We are selective — by design.

A small number of clients are accepted each year, following a rigorous qualification process and a track record on the type of transaction proposed. We partner for the long run, not for one‑off trades.

Scope & factors of engagement

  • We source opportunities tied to physical flows of commodities trading.
  • We pursue recurring transactions only — parties must carry a meaningful track record in the relevant category.
  • We decline one‑off transactions and parties not genuinely related to the underlying business in scope or scale.
  • Every engagement must connect to an existing operation at a reasonable scale for that operation.

Emphasis on risk and scalability

We place special emphasis on four parameters:

  • Safety
  • Transparency
  • Scalability
  • Compliance

Transactions must be clean on operational, credibility, AML and red‑flag grounds — with particular weight on AML. Once those risks are managed, scalability becomes the primary driver of engagement: we prefer transactions that can grow with the client or within the sector.
